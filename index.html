What is Craig Claw

Your personal AI command center

Craig Claw is a purpose-built personal operating system — a framework for channeling AI tools, mental models, and structured workflows into one coherent practice.

It's not another chatbot wrapper. It's the system I use every day to research markets, build products, accelerate learning, and stay sharp in a profession where the rate of change is the only constant.

Named in the spirit of OpenClaw — the idea that the most powerful tool you can build is one that extends your own capabilities — Craig Claw is specific to my context: wealth management, AI product development, and the disciplined pursuit of compounding knowledge.

Philosophy

Learning in the age of AI

"The illiterate of the 21st century will not be those who cannot read and write, but those who cannot learn, unlearn, and relearn." — Alvin Toffler

The most consequential shift in knowledge work isn't that AI can answer questions — it's that the cost of learning has collapsed. What used to require a course, a mentor, or a decade of trial and error can now be explored in an afternoon with the right tools and the right questions.

But speed without direction is just noise. Craig Claw exists because I believe the professionals who will thrive are those who build systems for compounding knowledge — not just consuming it.

01

Curiosity as infrastructure

Every question you ask an AI is a seed. Build a system to plant them deliberately, track what grows, and prune what doesn't.

02

Depth over breadth

AI makes it trivially easy to skim a hundred topics. The edge comes from going deep — developing intuition that no model can replicate.

03

Output as proof of understanding

Writing, building, teaching — these are how you convert information into knowledge. AI accelerates the loop, but you still have to close it.

04

Compound daily

A 1% improvement in your system each day means you're 37x better in a year. The discipline of daily refinement is the real competitive advantage.
